OUR FACILITIES

The hotel offers a comprehensive array of facilities designed to cater to the needs of travelers, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay.
Guests can unwind in well-appointed rooms equipped with modern amenities such as high-speed Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, and plush bedding.
The hotel features a state-of-the-art fitness center and a serene spa for relaxation and rejuvenation.
For dining, there are multiple on-site restaurants serving diverse cuisines, alongside a cozy bar for evening refreshments.
